What is Signal Integrity?

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Sochein aap ne WhatsApp pe voice note bheja — agar dusre side pe "haan?" "kya?" "phir se bhej!" na aaye, toh Signal Integrity theek hai! Signal bhi yahi hai — receiver ko sahi samajh aaya = SI clear hai.
HIGH SPEED — KAB TRACE TRANSMISSION LINE BANTA HAI?
Jab signal ki rise time itni fast ho ke signal trace ke aadhe se zyada travel kar le pehle hi edge complete ho — tab simple trace transmission line ban jata hai! Rule of thumb: L > Tr × v / 6 — FR4 pe 1ns rise time = agar trace 1 inch se lambi hai, transmission line samjho!
Trace length > (Tr × v) / 6 = TRANSMISSION LINE!

Tr = rise time, v ≈ 6 inches/ns (FR4). Example: 500ps rise time → 0.5 inch se upar = T-line!

Transmission Lines

SIMPLE DEFINITION
PCB ki trace highway jaisi hai — lekin high speed pe har 5 steps pe speed breaker aa jata hai! Simple copper wire R, L, C ka cluster ban jata hai. Road aam lagti hai, lekin underneath pura circus chal raha hai.
KYA HO JATA HAI? TRACE R,L,C CLUSTER KAISE BANTA HAI
R (Resistance): Copper wire bhi thora resist karta hai — jaise purani pipe se kam paani aata hai. Signal ki energy thori garmi mein zaya ho jati hai.
L (Inductance): Current flow karta hai toh aas paas chhota magnetic field banta hai — jaise fan on karo toh hawa feel hoti hai. Yeh field signal ko rokne ki koshish karta hai = speed breaker!
C (Capacitance): Trace neeche ground plane hai — dono ke beech FR4 (dielectric) hai. Yeh ek chhota capacitor ban jata hai — jaise do plates ke beech hawa. Signal ki energy thori charge mein store ho jati hai.
G (Conductance): FR4 perfect insulator nahi — thora thora leakage hota hai, jaise cracked pipe se paani tapakta rehta hai.

Result: Yeh sab mil ke har millimeter pe ek chhota circuit banate hain — aur poori trace ek lambi chain ban jati hai! Is liye high speed pe trace "wire" nahi rehti — transmission line ban jati hai!
HOW TO DESIGN IT RIGHT
Saturn PCB tool download karo, 5 mil width set karo FR4 ke liye. Ground plane ko unbroken rakhna — yehi woh 50Ω ka secret mantra hai!

Key Concepts

  • Distributed R, L, C, G model
  • Characteristic Impedance Z₀
  • Propagation delay & velocity
Z₀ = √(L/C)

v = 1/√(LC)

Reflection

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Gallery mein ball maara — wapas aake aap ki mara! Yehi reflection hai. Signal jab impedance change hit karta hai toh bounce back ho kar chaos create karta hai. Signal kehte hai "mujhe wapas jaane do!"
HOW TO FIX REFLECTIONS
Ek 33Ω resistor laga do source pe. Itna sasta hai lekin signal ko lakhon ka nuksan bachata hai! Trace width suddenly mat badalna, aur stubs se door raho. DDR clock = always series termination at CPU.

Causes

  • Impedance discontinuity
  • Unterminated traces
  • Vias and connectors
Γ = (Z_L - Z₀)/(Z_L + Z₀)

Impedance Matching

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Shoes ka size exactly fit hona chahiye — 42 ka foot pe 42 ka shoe. Loose hai toh giroge, tight hai toh dard hoga! Impedance bhi exactly match hona chahiye warna signal "trip" kar ke gir jata hai!
HOW TO ACHIEVE MATCHING
Stackup calculator se 50Ω set karo. USB/HDMI = 100Ω differential. Connector ke neeche cutout shapes — shoes ka perfect fit silwane jaisa!

When ZL = Z0, reflections are eliminated — maximum power transfer is achieved.

Crosstalk

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Exam mein bagal wala cheat kar raha hai aur aap us ki awaaz sun rahe ho — concentration kharab! Ek trace ka signal dusri trace mein chala jaye = Crosstalk. Trace jitni kareeb, utna zyada "cheating"!
HOW TO REDUCE CROSSTALK
3x trace width distance = Social Distancing! Ground guard trace beech mein = Mask pehnana! Clock kabhi data ke saath parallel mat bhagna — exam mein ek hi table pe mat baithna!

NEXT (Near-End)

Coupled noise at the source end. Adds constructively — typically the larger component.

FEXT (Far-End)

Coupled noise at the receiver end. Depends on trace length & signal edge rate.

Eye Diagrams

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Doctor ke paas eye test — aankh khuli hai toh signal healthy, band ho rahi hai toh "chashma lagao!" Eye diagram signal ki health checkup hai — bada khula aankh = signal fit hai!
HOW TO OPEN A CLOSED EYE
Closed eye? Chashma pehnao! = Crystal oscillator lagao (RC nahi). Decoupling caps chip ke paas rakhna. Edge rate slow karo series resistor se. Aur agar phir bhi nahi khula toh pre-emphasis ka "laser surgery" bhi hai!

Termination Strategies

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Story ke end mein Full Stop "." lagao — warna sentence kabhi khatam nahi hoga! Termination = signal ke end pe resistor lagana taake woh wapas na aaye aur ringing na kare.
WHICH ONE TO USE — REAL EXAMPLE
DDR clock: 33Ω series at CPU. PCIe/HDMI: 100Ω diff at receiver. Clock: AC termination (power bachata hai). Yaad rakho: Resistor JAHAN lagana hai WAHAN lagana hai — 500 mils door matlab compromise!

Common Methods

  • Series: Source-side resistor (most common)
  • Parallel: Receiver-side to VCC/GND
  • Thevenin: Split termination to VCC & GND
  • AC: Capacitor in series — saves DC power
R_term ≈ Z₀

EMI & EMC

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Raat 3 baje DJ baja rahe ho aur padosi ne police bulayi — yeh EMI hai! Aapka board shor machata hai. EMC = padosi ko disturb mat karo, aur khud bhi kisi ke shor se pareshan mat ho. Achha neighbour bano!
HOW TO PASS EMI TESTING
Ground plane = aapka bodyguard. Ferrite bead = noise ka maskan. Stitching via = boundary wall. Inke bina board testing fail! Switching loops chhota rakho — decoupling caps chip ke moo pe lagao!

EMI Sources

  • High di/dt switching currents
  • Impedance mismatches
  • Loop antenna effects

Mitigation

  • Proper stackup & reference planes
  • Controlled impedance routing
  • Shielding & filtering

Power Integrity

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Jab aap AC on karte ho aur light dim ho jati hai — yeh Power Integrity ka issue hai! Har chip ko clean 3.3V chahiye, fluctuation nahi chalega. "Sab ko ek jaisi roti milegi" — yeh PI ka motto hai!
HOW TO FIX POWER INTEGRITY
100nF + 10µF caps chip ke moo pe — 2mm se zyada door nahi! Bulk caps (47-100µF) board ke gate pe. Solid power/ground planes — split mat karna! PDN simulate karo, flat response below Z_target chahiye.

PDN (Power Distribution Network) impedance must be below the target impedance across the entire frequency range to maintain stable voltage delivery.

Z_target = ΔV_allowed / ΔI_max

Via Effects in High-Speed Design

SIMPLE DEFINITION
Highway chal rahe the aur achanak speed breaker + pothole aa gaya — yeh Via hai! Layer change karte waqt signal ko "dhachka" lagta hai. Via = signal ka laacha (bump) jo mushkil mein daalta hai!
HOW TO MINIMIZE VIA DAMAGE
Back-drill = via ka extra hissa kaat do! Ground return via = bodyguard har signal via ke saath. Micro-vias = aam se chhota, kam nuksan. Critical nets pe via kam = kam speed breaker = smooth drive!

Key Issues

  • Parasitic Capacitance: ~0.3-0.8 pF per via — slows edges
  • Parasitic Inductance: ~0.5-1.0 nH — causes resonance
  • Impedance Discontinuity: 10-30% local impedance shift
  • Stub Resonance: Unused via portion acts as antenna

Solutions

  • Back-drill stubs to reduce resonance
  • Use micro-vias for minimal parasitics
  • Add return-path vias nearby (stitching)
